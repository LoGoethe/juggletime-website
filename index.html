About

Welcome to JuggleTime — the circus experience that blends expert skill with joyful spectacle. Whether dazzling a festival crowd, lighting up a school assembly, or bringing laughter to a local block party, Logan Goethe delivers performances full of energy, elegance, and flair.

Logan Goethe is a seasoned juggler from Chattanooga, TN. His journey into circus arts began over 15 years ago, driven by a relentless passion for creative expression that led him to perform on stages all over the world. These days, his artistic path weaves through juggling, partner acrobatics, clowning, physical theater, music, dance, and more as he refines his craft both onstage and in the classroom. Above all, Logan is fiercely dedicated to sharing the joy of juggling, the power of playfulness, and the welcoming spirit of the circus world.
